Monster Cleaner

Monster Cleaner is a game developed in the context of the 2021 Unijam, a game jam organized every year by 3 schools : Télécom SudParis (the school I attended), ENSIIE and Bobigny IUT.

Monster Cleaner is a 2D side-on shooter game, where you play as a garbage collector tasked with cleaning a monster nest in the sewers. After each wave of enemies, a new and more powerful weapon will spawn. Grab it and enjoy its new special effects ! A friendly raccoon will recover the old weapon you discarded.
The twist ? The innocent-looking creature will wait for you at the end and fight you with all the weapons you discarded !
Will you grab every new weapon and empower him, or only use you base weapon and make him defenceless ?

You can try it out on itch.io and visit the Unijam 2021 page.

Our team consisted of 6 persons, and I was the graphist : I made every sprite used in the game by hand. I also helped designing the gameplay in the early phased, and animated several sprites in Unity. We managed to balance a steady workflow and a healthy sleep schedule, and our efficient teamwork allowed us to win the third place out of 11 games developed.


The various weapons at your disposal

Assault rifle : your base weapon

Laser sniper : shoots bullets that pass through platforms

Bubble shooter : shoots bullets that bounces on walls and platforms

Energy cannon : shoots explosive bullets
